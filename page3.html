My Sleep and Mental Health Plan

Sleep

Sleeping is very important for your overall health. You can be doing everything right, but if you don't get a good nights rest, it could affect your entire day. Here's my plan for better sleep:

  1. Set a strict bedtime and wake up time.
  2. Wear an eye mask to limit light exposure.
  3. Read before bed

These goals are achievable, I am not too stressed about my sleeping habits. I have turned a lot of these goals into habits. I will now show how I will continue to keep these habits going:

  1. Set a strict bedtime and wake up time.

    • I will set an alarm on my phone at the same time every morning.
    • Calculate 8 hours of sleep based on the wake up time.
    • Drink a cup of hot water with lemon as soon as I wake up.

  2. Wear an eye mask to limit light exposure.

    • Keep the eye mask on my pillow to remind me to wear it.
    • Wear castor oil on my eyelashes before bed.

  3. Read before bed

    • Keep a book by my bedside to read before sleep.

I do most of these habits consistently. None of these habits are too difficult for me to maintain. I will just be adding another habit to my routine. Now it's time to focus on my mental health:

Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. I feel like the biggest helper to all of habit building is discipline. I will need to strengthen my mind. Here are my goals:

  1. Keep a positive mindset.
  2. Spend 10 minutes each day meditating.
  3. Give myself grace

These goals look easy at first, but the mind works in mysterious ways. I will need to be patient with myself. Here's the ways I will prioiritize these goals:

  1. Keep a positive mindset.

    • When I'm doing workouts that are hard, I need to remember that it's part of the process.

  2. Spend 10 minutes each day meditating.

    • I will use a meditation app, since this is new to me.

  3. Give myself grace.

    • When I make mistakes, I will remind myself that it's part of the journey.

It's very important to have a positive mindset throughout your journey. It really makes a difference in how you invision your goals.
